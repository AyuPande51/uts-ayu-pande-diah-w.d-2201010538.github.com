One of the tourist attractions in Bali is very popular with foreign tourists and local tourists who visit this village. Penglipuran Village has a traditional Balinese village structure. You can feel the calm and coolness in the village which has been named the third cleanest village in the world, after Mawlynnong Village in India and Giethoorn in the Netherlands. Following are the origins, geographical conditions,and tourist villages of Penglipuran Village in Bali. Stories in the community say that Penglipuran Village was a gift from the King of Bangli to the people who fought against the Gianyar Kingdom. The people of Penglipuran Village hold hundreds of years of ancestral traditions. In addition to the system of government regulations, they still apply traditional law in society, namely awig-awig. The ability to maintain this tradition is what makes Penglipuran Village unique. They uphold customs, the value of kinship, local wisdom based on the concept of Tri Hitha Karana.
