A special place for those who want to discover the beauty and instagramable tourist spots in Bali. For photographers,of course it will be the next interesting place as a place to take pre-wedding photos. A number of certain spots or points present dramatic beauty, seem very romantic, especially if you are traveling with your partner. The atmosphere of the forest is calm and comfortable, and the coolness makes you feel at home for long in this place. This natural recreation area is ideal for all people, both for families and children. Apart from being a recreational destination, there are footpaths or village roads that are located in the bamboo forest, residents often use it for morning or evening sports, this place isalready popular with local residents.

Another charm offered by Penglipuran Village is a cultural festival called Penglipuran Village Festival. This event is usually held at the end of the year with a series of various activities, ranging from traditional Balinese dress parades, Barong Ngelawang, cultural arts parades, and many more. With all the natural beauty, cultural uniqueness, and traditional traditions that are still thick, of course you will be satisfied when touring Penglipuran Village. You will really feel the atmosphere of an authentic and still beautiful Balinese village
